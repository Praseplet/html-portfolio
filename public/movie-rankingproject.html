My Top 5 Favourite Movies

Amelie

My favourite movie from childhood. Captivating and beautiful story of a young parisian woman who trys to make lives of the people she meet more hopeful.

Life Is Beautiful

Great movie, starring legendary Roberto Benigni.

The Pianist

The story of a young pianist during the World War II.

Old Boy

Weird but great movie, and also one of the most famous movies from South Korea.

Once Upon A Time in Hollywood

My favourite Tarantino's movie. Told as a tale, what would have happened in an alternative universe.
